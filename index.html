Sentinel Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Thank you for using Sentinel.

At Sentinel, protecting your privacy is just as important as helping protect your device. Sentinel Version 1 is designed to perform its security functions locally on your device whenever possible.

Information We Collect

Sentinel Version 1 does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information for sale or marketing purposes.

Local Processing

Sentinel processes security information locally on your device. Reports and scan results remain on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Sentinel does not transmit your personal information to our servers.

Data Sharing

Sentinel does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

If you choose to export or share a report, you control where that information is sent.

Permissions

Sentinel may request Android permissions that are necessary for app features to function correctly. These permissions are only used for their intended purpose.

Data Security

Sentinel is designed with security and privacy in mind. While we work to help protect your device, no security software can guarantee protection against every possible threat.

Children's Privacy

Sentinel is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Sentinel adds new features or services. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
